A2 Summer Research Project

This project is for the current year 12 students who will begin A2 Media Studies in September 2014.

The purpose of this summer project is to help you begin preparing for the range of work which is required during A2, at this point do not worry about whether it will fit into the exam case studies or the critical investigation.

  1. Find an article, or TV programme or podcast from an authoritative source (see the list PH gave you), which explores, rather than just reports on a media issue (eg the Laurie Penny article PH gave you).
  2. Take a quotation from the article which sums up the point the writer of the article is making about the media (e.g. ‘Sex sells, but sexism sells even better.’).
  3. Using a range of primary research (i.e. three or four media products, eg a film, a TV programme, a magazine article, a computer game), explore the extent to which this statement is true. Refer in detail to your examples.

You should aim to write 750-1000 words. Write a conclusion summing up your own thoughts on the statement you chose and the extent to which it is true.

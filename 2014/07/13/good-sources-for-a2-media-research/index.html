Good Sources for A2 Media Research

Mr Hargood has produced a list of good sources for A2 Media Research. In order to make it easier for you we have made each of these sources a direct link to the relevant website.

Newspapers

  • The Guardian / Observer
  • The Independent
  • The Daily Telegraph
  • The Times (Subscription)

Weeklies

  • The New Statesman
  • The Spectator

TV

  • Newsnight (BBC2)
  • Newswatch (BBC News)
  • C4 News
  • Documentaries

Radio Podcasts

  • The Media Podcast (PPM)
  • The Media Show (BBC Radio 4)
  • Tech Weekly (The Guardian)
  • Trending (BBC World Service)
  • Saturday Review (BBC Radio 4)
  • The Film Programme (BBC Radio 4)
  • Feedback (BBC Radio 4)
  • Documentaries

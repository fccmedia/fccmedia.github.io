Welcome ...

Welcome to the official website for the Felpham Community College Media Studies Department.

When we launched our Twitter feed a few months ago, we hoped it would increase our ability to communicate with our students; however now we want to take this further.

While the site will become fully operational from the beginning of the new school year, you can consider the next few months the beta testing phase.

While this website will not replace Moodle for assignment guidance and submission, it will act as the central hub for all of the information we want to share with you. It is going to be work in progress, so remember to let us know what you would like to see being put on the site.

You can always send us a message on Twitter, via E-mail or just drop into the Media Centre for a chat.

Mr Manning
